‚ùì What is ERP?

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) is a type of software used by organizations to manage and integrate the core parts of their business. An ERP system helps businesses streamline processes by collecting, storing, managing, and interpreting data from various business activities in one centralized platform.

ERP software integrates functions such as:

  • Sales and Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
  • Inventory and Supply Chain Management
  • Human Resources and Payroll
  • Finance and Accounting
  • Manufacturing and Production
  • Project Management

By consolidating business processes into a single system, ERP helps organizations improve efficiency, reduce errors, make better decisions, and enhance collaboration across departments.
